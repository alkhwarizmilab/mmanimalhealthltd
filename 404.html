M Animal Health, a sister concern of Munni Enterprise was established in 2015 to ensure world class nutritional products and feed additives, comprises an extensive catalogue of pharmacological products, pesticide, feed premix, nutritional formulations & other feed additives of livestock, poultry & aquaculture.
